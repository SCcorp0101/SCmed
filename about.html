About Us

As a dynamic startup in the medical devices industry, Sc Med is dedicated to revolutionizing healthcare with cutting-edge technology and personalized solutions. Based in Algiers, Algeria, we specialize in the distribution, selling, and manufacturing of high-quality medical devices, including auto refractometers and more. At Sc Med, we believe in combining innovation with reliability. Our mission is to provide not only advanced medical equipment but also customized software solutions and tables designed to meet the unique needs of our clients. Whether you're a healthcare professional seeking precise instruments or a facility in need of tailored solutions, we are here to support you with excellence and expertise. Our Vision To lead in providing innovative medical solutions that enhance patient care and streamline healthcare operations.
